Young Artist Lavani

Artist Lavani comes from an underprivileged background and studies in Class XII in a Government school. She joined Nanhi Kashtiyan in 2017 when she was in class IV.

She was inspired by the Artwork of Mr. Roshan Sahai who is a Sketch Artist and the founder of NGO Nanhi Kashtiyan. She expresses her interest in learning sketch art. Here her Art journey starts, under Roshan Art, she is being given art training for 3 years, she was provided with bets artistic tool available in the market, it is her dedication and passion that she has made it, now she is known as Artist Lavani, she has become a true artist with values who is now inspiring many children in her society and school.

She has now become a professional artist, she is making money from her Art skill and supporting her family. If you wish to connect or place an order for sketch, please reach out to her Instagram handle @luvny_artsy.
