Our Success Stories

KANYADAAN.

Nanhi Kashtiyan, a compassionate and dedicated NGO, uplifts society in multifaceted ways. Among its noble endeavors, assisting young girls during their marriages stands out. Here's how they contribute:

  1. Education: Nanhi Kashtiyan empowers girls with knowledge and life skills, ensuring they navigate marriage confidently.
  2. Breaking Stereotypes: They advocate for love marriages and interfaith unions, challenging societal norms.
  3. Financial Independence: By teaching financial literacy and vocational skills, they enable girls to contribute meaningfully.
  4. Health and Well-being: Awareness camps educate girls about reproductive health and hygiene.
  5. Emotional Support: Counseling helps brides handle pre-wedding jitters and marital challenges.
  6. Dignity and Respect: Nanhi Kashtiyan fights against dowry demands and discrimination.
  7. Community Celebrations: They organize joyous community weddings, reinforcing unity.
