WORD FROM FOUNDER

ROSHAN SAHAI

We are extremely delighted to share that Nanhi Kashtiyan has been playing a pivotal role of being an enabler across all segments of the society in varied fields such as Child Education, Child Rights, Women Empowerment, Health & Environment since 2013.

We believe EDUCATION is the backbone of a society and hence it is our Core Focus Area. The Importance of Education cannot be stressed more in present day and age whereby we are progressing towards a knowledge based economy. We can eradicate poverty, reduce the gulf of inequality by promoting Education for All.

Education touches lives in more than one ways, we aim to target the young and impressionable minds and free them of any kind of biases. The demographics of India is young but a huge percentage of them remain under privileged. Hence, this gap needs to be filed in at the earliest. We at Nanhi Kashityan are committed to this cause.

We have collaborated with other NGOs having similar objectives, CSR dept at large Corporates and mobilizing local communities to provide free Education to the underprivileged children with basic reading, writing and numerical ability with subtle tips on developing soft and social skills and enables the children to participate in decisions that affect his/her lives.

With all the support from our well-wishers and patrons, we shall strive to put in our best efforts in all possible ways to bring about a lasting change among those under-privileged but opportunity starved children so as to provide them with a level playing field when they join the main-stream society later-on in their life.
