INSPIRATION BEHIND NANHI KASHTIYAN

WORD FROM REKHA MA'AM .

Three decades ago, Mrs. Rekha Anand devotedly pursued the goal of providing education and aid to under-privileged children in the Dwarka locality of Delhi. Among the many children whose lives she touched, Mr. Roshan Sahai stands out. Mrs. Rekha’s role as a teacher, mentor and guide motivated him to launch Nanhi Kastiyan to carry forward the noble goal Mrs. Rekha Anand had dedicated herself to. She describes her motivation behind working to help students in the following words, “Bachhon ko shiksha miley, bachhe agey badhe, isse badhiye baat aur kya ho sakti hai.”

She recalls that her experience working with children, providing them with essentials such as food and clothes was an entirely fulfilling one. Watching the children learn and progress under her guidance inspired her further. Reminiscing fondly, she believes that the inquisitiveness and desire to learn she saw in the children became a driving force for her.

She also recollects the challenges she encountered in making a meaningful difference in the lives of children she reached out to. According to her, giving back to society is an honourable virtue.

Hence, she expresses pride over Mr. Roshan Sahai and the initiatives of Nanhi Kastiyan as she adheres to the belief that there is no greater good deed than working for the upliftment of children.

Contributions can fund scholarships, school supplies, and extracurricular programs, fostering a supportive learning environment.

These donations empower children to achieve their full potential and build a brighter future.
