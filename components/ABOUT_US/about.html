Nanhi Kashtiyan

Nanhi Kashtiyan is a registered body under Society Act, 1860 bearing Registration Number: S/RS/DW(SW)/1630/2019, working towards spreading free education among slum children in the neighborhood of Dwarka since 2013.

At Nanhi Kashtiyan, we actively aim to ensure inclusivity for underprivileged children within the progressing society. Education is known to change the course of history, providing a unique experience and identity to each and every child. Therefore, we realized that in order to bring meaningful change to society, it becomes imperative to educate these young pupils and free their minds from biases.

OUR VISION

To inspire society in a way that unprivileged children have access to resources just like every other child, enabling them to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives.

OUR MISSION

To narrow the gap between different sections of society by educating, enabling, and empowering every child.

OUR MOTTO

To nourish the minds and bodies of our children because they are the future change-makers. With adequate resource allocation, every mind has the ability to achieve the impossible and bring about positive change in society.

EDUCATING . ENABLING . EMPOWERING

Our success lies in the aim of fulfilling the 3E’s. Thus:

  • We ensure regular classroom teaching to our children along with several co-curricular activities
    such as motivational movies for children.
  • Monthly events in the form of speaker sessions and workshops by prolific speakers on topics
    like good touch-bad touch, self-defense, menstrual hygiene, etc.
