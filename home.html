NANHI KASHTIYAN
EDUCATING.ENABLING.EMPOWERING

Nanhi Kashtiyan is a registered body under Society Act, 1860 bearing Registration Number: S/RS/DW(SW)/1630/2019, working towards spreading of free education among slum children in the neighborhood of Dwarka since 2013. We actively aim to ensure inclusivity of the underprivileged children with the progressing society. At Nanhi Kashtiyan, we actively aim to ensure inclusivity of the underprivileged children with the progressing society. Education is known to change the course of histo- ry and it provides a unique experience and identity to each and every child. Therefore, we realized that in order to bring a meaningful change in the society, it becomes imperative to educate these young pupils and free their mind of biases.We have 3 Learning Centres in Dwarka, Delhi and have a combined strength of more than 352 kids.
